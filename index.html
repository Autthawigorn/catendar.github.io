Thank you for using Catendar! Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle your data while using our app.

1. Information We Collect

Catendar does not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information (PII).

  • Local Data Storage: All your calendar data and settings are stored locally on your device using SwiftData and, optionally, CloudKit for syncing between your devices. This data is not accessible by us or any third parties.
  • Payment Information: In-app purchases are processed securely through Apple’s App Store. We do not collect or have access to your payment details.

2. How We Use Your Data

Catendar processes your data solely to provide the app's functionality:

  • To manage and display your plans.
  • To enable optional data syncing via iCloud (CloudKit).

Your data is never shared with us or any third parties.

3. Sharing of Information

Catendar does not share your data with any third parties.

4. Data Security

Catendar is designed with security in mind:

  • Your data is stored locally on your device and in your iCloud account (if enabled).
  • Access to your iCloud data is governed by Apple’s privacy and security policies.

While we strive to keep your data secure, we recommend safeguarding your device with a strong passcode and enabling two-factor authentication for your Apple ID.

5. Your Rights

As Catendar does not collect or store your personal information, there are no data rights to exercise within the app. You retain full control of your data stored on your device or in iCloud.

6. Children’s Privacy

Catendar does not knowingly collect, use, or share data from children under the age of 13. As no user accounts are required, the app is suitable for users of all ages.
